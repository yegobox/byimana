Our Facilities

We are one of the best well equipped schools in the country and this facilitates our students and make the learning process more enjoyable.

The Library

Our school Library termed as "The English Berger Library" is where students can find different hard and soft E-books, do preps or conduct research

Smart classes

Esc BYIMANA is equipped with smart classes with high speed internet connection. Each class has it's own projector and whiteboard

Science labs

Esc Byimana has one of the most well equipped science labs in Rwanda. We have three labs for Chemistry, Physics and Biology

Computer labs

We have a total of 127 computers and two well equipped Computer labs with fast internet for research and Computer Science for MPC students

Video conference room

Esc Byimana has many collaborators abroad and students share knowledge with them directly through video conference.the room is equipped with fast internet and necessary ICT tools for this process

The Champagnat hall

Serves enough space for students to relax, host shows and different events organised by the school or the Government. The hall also serves as a place for study purposes and debates
