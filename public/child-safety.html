Ecole des Sciences Byimana supports children through its Child Support Program, focusing on health, talents, and safety. This comprehensive program ensures students receive holistic care and opportunities to thrive in various aspects of their lives. The school places a strong emphasis on student health. Regular medical check-ups and access to healthcare services are provided to monitor and maintain the physical well-being of the students. Health education programs teach children about nutrition, hygiene, and healthy lifestyles, ensuring they make informed choices that benefit their long-term health.

The school is committed to creating a safe and secure environment for all students. Comprehensive safety policies are in place, including regular safety drills, secure campus facilities, and trained staff to handle emergencies. Additionally, anti-bullying programs and counseling services support students' emotional and psychological well-being, ensuring a nurturing and protective atmosphere.
