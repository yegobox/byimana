SPORTS AT ESC BYIMANA

Sports are essential for the good functioning of our bodies enabling us to fully focus and prevent diseases while maintaining fit bodies, We all know that sports are essential for good performance in class thus must be emphasized on. Our school realised that earlier on thus providing all possible support to help promote sport activities and help those who are passionate about certain sports like Soccer, Basketball and many more. The time for sport is compulsory for every student and has to participate in one or many of the sports available.Sport is also taught as a lesson in the course Physical Education and sports (PES)

VARIOUS SPORTS AT ESC BYIMANA

Esc Byimana provides enough sports facilities and a variety of choises from
which students can participate.

Football

With a large community of admires, our school provided enough playgrounds and football facilities including balls, outfits and much more

Volleyball

One of the most popular and admired sports here. Students are provided with enough support like coaches and much more

Basketball

The Esc Byimana Basketball team is a dominant contestant in the southern province and beyond, with lots of support from the school

Passionate about Martial Arts? We have a great Karate Team with Great Instructors that will help you get started and excell further

Want to burn fat and get fit fast? We have a great community of athletes that can help you start athletism real quick with all resources that you'll need.

Ping Pong

All the way from England, for all of you that admire Table Tennis / Ping Pong, We have all necessary facilities that will help you enjoy this great sports

Amusing Games

Amusing games is a great sports for all those who like rope skipping, strategy and mind games to both develop your physical and mental abilities
